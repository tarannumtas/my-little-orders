What Haul does

Built for the serial shopper

Track Every Order
Log purchases from Daraz, Shajghor, Chaldal, Facebook sellers — anywhere. Store the item, price, source, and ETA in one place.
Monitor Delivery
Set your ETA and track status from ordered to shipped to received. Update with one tap from your dashboard.
Post-Delivery Reviews
Rate quality, satisfaction, and decide if you'd buy again. Never forget which seller disappointed you.
Photo Gallery
Upload multiple product photos per order — see what you ordered vs. what actually arrived.
Spending Insights
Live snapshot of your total spend, COD pending, and how many orders are still in transit.
Private & Secure
All data stays in your own browser. Nothing uploaded to any server — your shopping history is yours alone.
